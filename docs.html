Nimo serves as a gateway to games hosted by external providers. All games are hosted and maintained by third-party providers, and we are not responsible for the content, functionality, or availability of external games.

Each external game may have its own terms of service and privacy policies. Users are subject to the terms of both Nimo and the respective game providers.
